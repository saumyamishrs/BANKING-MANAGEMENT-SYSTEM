About IERT Bank

Your Partner in Financial Growth Since 1985

Our Story

Established in 1985, IERT Bank has been at the forefront of financial innovation and customer satisfaction. Over the years, we have evolved from a single branch to a robust network of banking solutions that empower individuals and businesses alike.

Our mission is to provide secure, innovative, and inclusive financial services that drive growth and prosperity for everyone. With a dedicated team of professionals and a customer-first approach, we have become a trusted name in the banking industry.

Key Milestones

1985 - Foundation

The bank was established with the vision of delivering trustworthy financial services to the local community.

1995 - Digital Banking Introduced

Pioneered online banking solutions, becoming one of the first in the region to do so.

2010 - Nationwide Expansion

Expanded operations across the country, opening over 200 branches nationwide.

2020 - Sustainability Initiatives

Launched green banking initiatives, emphasizing eco-friendly practices and community upliftment.
